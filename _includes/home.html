Siq Liquid Orange is a delicious and juicy fruit blend primarily composed of apricot and peach with just enough vanilla and cream to bind the flavors and soften the crisp ripe fruits.

Siq Liquids Yellow will tickle your tongue with a light tart yet sweet lemon flavor and a cookie flavor so amazing you can feel the crunch in your mouth.

Siq Liquids Mint Green is a complex blend of cucumber and mint with undertones of melon. this is an incredibly refreshing flavor that gives you the sensation of a sweet crunchy cucumber.

Siq Liquids Purple will fill your mouth with a smooth creamy vanilla flavor reminiscent of many people's favorite dessert. On exhale you will taste crystallized sugar and maybe even a light strawberry.

Siq Liquids White will bring you back to your childhood when your mom packed individual cakes in your lunch. Just like eating that cake was the best part of your day, so is vaping this flavor.

Siq Liquid Black is a mouthwatering mix of rich and sweet chocolate flavors doused in sweet cream. This is the chocolate flavor you've been searching for.
